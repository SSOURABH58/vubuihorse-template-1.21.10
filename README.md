## Why This Mod Exists

If you've watched Vu Bui's "LATEST GAME DROP DATE REVEAL! | MINECRAFT MONTHLY" video, you know exactly where this is going. When discussing the new Mounts of Mayhem update, Vu had a brilliantly ridiculous idea:

And you know what? That's exactly what we're doing. Because why should llamas have all the fun? It's time horses got their moment to shine... literally, with fire.

## What This Mod Does

This mod transforms every horse in Minecraft into a majestic, fireball-spitting beast. Here's what you're getting:

### ðŸ”¥ Fireball Mechanics
- **All horses can spit fireballs** - Uses the classic ghast fireball animations and mechanics
- **No block destruction** - Fireballs deal entity damage only, your builds are safe!
- **Fire spread enabled** - Things will catch fire, because that's just more fun

### ðŸŽ¯ Behavior System
- **Passive by default** - Horses remain peaceful until provoked
- **Defensive spitting** - Hit a horse and it'll remember YOU specifically and spit fireballs at you
- **Player-only targeting** - Horses only target players who hurt them, not other mobs
- **Wild horse aggression** - Untamed horses will occasionally spit fireballs at nearby hostile mobs (not players)
- **Tamed horses are peaceful** - Once tamed, horses only spit on player command

### ðŸŽ® Player Controls
- **Keybind for tamed horses** - Once you've tamed and saddled a horse, press R (customizable) to make it shoot fireballs
- **Aim-based shooting** - Fireballs go where you're looking, not straight up!
- **Cooldown display** - See the cooldown timer at the top-left of your screen (like potion effects) with a fire icon
- **Customizable keybind** - Change the key in Controls > VuBui Horse section
- **Cooldown system** - Random cooldown between 5-10 seconds (configurable) to keep things balanced

### ðŸ§¬ Genetic Trait
- **Hereditary ability** - The fireball-spitting trait passes to offspring when breeding
- **Build your army** - Breed an entire stable of fire-breathing war horses

## Installation

1. Make sure you have NeoForge installed for Minecraft 1.21.x
2. Download the VuBui Horse mod jar
3. Place it in your `mods` folder
4. Launch Minecraft and enjoy your new fire-breathing companions

## Configuration

The mod creates a config file at `config/vubuihorse-common.toml` where you can adjust:
- Fireball cooldown minimum time (default: 5 seconds)
- Fireball cooldown maximum time (default: 10 seconds)
- Fireball damage
- Fire spread behavior
